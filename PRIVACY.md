# CSV Studio — Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** January 2026

## Introduction

CSV Studio ("the App") is a Windows application developed by LabsDev.  
This Privacy Policy explains how the App handles user data.

## TL;DR

✅ CSV Studio works **100% offline**.  
✅ Your CSV files are **never** uploaded to any server.  
✅ We **do not** collect personal data.  
✅ We **do not** use analytics or tracking.

## Data we collect

**We collect no personal data.**

CSV Studio does not:
- Send your files or their content to any server
- Collect telemetry, analytics, or usage statistics
- Track your actions inside the app
- Use cookies or similar technologies
- Share data with third parties

## How CSV Studio uses files

All file processing happens **locally on your device**.

When you load a CSV:
- The file is read from your local disk
- All operations (detection, cleanup, export) run on your computer
- The output is saved to your local disk

No file content ever leaves your computer.

## App settings

CSV Studio stores user preferences (theme, preview row limit) in local Windows settings.

These settings:
- Remain on your device
- Are not transmitted anywhere
- Can be reset from the Settings page

## Permissions

CSV Studio requests the following Windows capabilities:
- **Internet (Client):** Reserved for future optional features. Currently unused.

CSV Studio does **not** request access to:
- Documents library, Pictures library, or similar
- Webcam, microphone, location, contacts
- Any other sensitive system resource

## Children's privacy

CSV Studio does not knowingly collect data from anyone, including children under 13.

Updates will be published in this repository with a new "Last updated" date.
