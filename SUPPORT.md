## FAQ

**Q: Does CSV Studio send my data to the cloud?**  
A: No. CSV Studio works **100% offline**. Your files never leave your computer.

**Q: What file formats are supported?**  
A: CSV (.csv) and tab-delimited (.txt). Export to CSV and JSON.

**Q: How big can my CSV files be?**  
A: CSV Studio handles files of any size. The preview shows the first 1,000 rows.

**Q: Can I batch process multiple files?**  
A: Yes! Use the Batch tab to apply the same operations to dozens of CSV files at once.

**Q: Is CSV Studio free?**  
A: There is a free version with limited rows and a Pro version with full features.
